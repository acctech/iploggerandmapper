Academica

Academica showcases the research, insights and work of teachers and leaders in the nation-wide Australian Christian College network of schools.

The journal is intended to cover a broad range of topics such as Christian education, academic improvement, online education, character development, student and teacher wellbeing, professional educational issues and education technology.

Academica’s content demonstrates the ACC school network’s commitment to pedagogical improvement, contemporary research and exemplary teaching practice.

Journal Contents:

  • evidence-based educational research
  • theoretical articles
  • opinion pieces
  • case studies
  • event recounts
  • alumni profiles
  • book reviews
